The Prime Design Cycle

5. Design Definition

Design- A plan or drawing produced to show the look and function or workings of a building, garment, or other object before it is made.

6. Technology Definition

Technology- Technology is machinery and devices developed from scientific knowledge. (anything made by man is technology)

7. An Outline of the Prime Design Stages

Problem

Problem is the design brief. It tells us the issue that our design has to solve. The problem is the opposite to the design because the design is the solution and is normally very brief just depicting the function and athstetics of the Final Product.

Research

Reasearch is looking at what has been done previously to try to solve the issue and what could improve your design. Normally a idea or design is formed from numerous chunks of other designs because imagination is the abilty to combine existing technology.

Ideas

Ideas is the actual designing of our product and is done usually using pencil and paper sketchs. It is what we will build that we design in the ideas phase.

Make

Make is when you build the idea and bring it to life. It melts from a design into a tangable object that can preform whatever the design situation requires it to do.

Evaluate

Evaluate is the easiest stage in the PRIME process. It is simpily taking the final product and reviewing what made it good.
